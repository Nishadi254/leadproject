================================================================================
METRICS FORMULAS
================================================================================

Conversion Rate:
  (Converted Leads / Qualified Leads) × 100
  Example: 5 / 20 = 25%

Response Time:
  Average time from creation to first contact
  Example: 2.3h = 2 hours 30 minutes

Monthly Growth:
  ((This Month - Last Month) / Last Month) × 100
  Example: (45 - 40) / 40 = +12.5%

Percentage of Total:
  (Status Count / Total Leads) × 100
  Example: 30 / 100 = 30%
